RLC Computers

Calgary PC Building & Consultation

What is it?

Located in Calgary, I offer both paid PC building for those in the area and free PC part picking consultation. If you're wondering what parts to pick, just send over your budget and I can help!

How does it work?

If you want a PC built for you, you can give me the already-purchased parts (preferred) or send the money over for it to be purchased, and I'll build it for you for a base price of $100. Either I come pick up the parts from you or you can drop them off to me, and I'll get to work! I have no problem giving you my phone number if you want to stay in touch, and I'll make sure to keep you updated.

Why?

Building a PC yourself or getting someone locally to do it is generally cheaper than buying a pre-built from a major corporation. Not to say that they're bad, but if you're willing to help out a local and up-and-coming business, feel free to contact me! I'd love to help out people trying to save money on a new computer or someone trying to understand more about PC building. I'm 18 and I have a genuine passion for building computers. I built my first computer back in July with a friend and I had a great time doing it. There's just something about putting all the pieces together and making it work that I love, and any excuse for me to work on computers is a good excuse in my book.

Contact

If you wish to contact me for a PC build or consultation, email me directly at rlccomputerscontact@gmail.com! Make sure to let me know what you care about: What's your budget? Do you care about looks? If so, what aesthetic are you aiming for? What do you plan to use the PC for? Do you prefer air-cooled or water-cooled (aio)? I'll make sure to ask any other questions if there's any information missing so I can best understand what you're looking for. I look forward to doing business with you and appreciate your consideration!
